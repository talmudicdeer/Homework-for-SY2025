### Pre-Lab Questions

**1. In your own words, define selective permeability.** Selective permeability is the property by which membranes that are selectively permeable will allow some ions and molecules to pass through, but not others.

**2. Explain why all particles cannot passively diffuse across cell membranes.** Because the molecular composition of cell membranes, as well as that of the particles themselves, prohibits this.

**3. What is the energy that powers diffusion?** Kinetic energy.
