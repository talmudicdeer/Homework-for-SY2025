**What is a path in a graph?**
A sequence of edges that connects a series of vertices, where no vertex is repeated.

**What are the in-degree and out-degree in a directed graph?**
The incoming and outcoming edges from a vertex, respectively.

**What is an adjacency matrix?**
A square matrix that shows edge connections between vertices.

**What is an adjacency list?**
The same thing as above, but in the form of a list instead of a matrix.

**What is the difference between a directed and an undirected graph?**
Edge direction in a directed graph is monodirectional, and doesn't exist in an undirected graph.
